Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. How to make Lasagna Step by Step

    1. Make the Meat Sauce
    2. Cook the Noodles
    3. Make the ricotta mixture
    4. Layer the Lasagna according to the receipe instructions
    5. cover with foil and bake
    6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

  2. How to Layer Lasagna

    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta Mixture
    4. Mozzarella Slices
    5. Meat Sauce
    6. Parmesan Cheese
    7. Repeat the layers then top with remaining cheese.
